# Clinical and biological variables of interest in CLL

## Clinical variables

- age
- sex
- Rai stage
- Binet stage
- hemoglobin
- platelets
- lymphocytes
- lymph nodes
- splenomegaly
- hepatomegaly
- constitutional symptoms
- infections
- autoimmune complications

## Biological and genetic variables

- IGHV status
- TP53 mutation
- del(17p)
- del(11q)
- trisomy 12
- del(13q)
- beta-2 microglobulin
- LDH
- flow cytometry markers
- transcriptomic or multi-omic variables if available

## Possible endpoints

- progression
- time-to-first-treatment
- treatment response
- progression-free survival
- risk group
- aggressive transformation
- longitudinal clinical trajectory
